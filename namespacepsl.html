    nop_init_t psl::nop_init constexpr
    #include <types.hpp>

    "no-operation" initalization tag. Used by constainers and wrappers to force specific construction behaviour of contained types.

    Warning

    this signifies "no initialization at all", not "initialize with nothing". This means an object will not even be constructed. As example, an array<> type will allocate memory for objects, but not construct them at all with this tag. You will have to in-place construct them after if you wish to have access to non-trivial types (or don't like undefined behaviour).
