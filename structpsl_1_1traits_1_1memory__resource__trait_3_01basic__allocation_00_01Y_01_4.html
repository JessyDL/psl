template<typename Y>
alloc_results<void> psl::traits::memory_resource_trait<basic_allocation, Y><Y>::allocate(size_t size, size_t alignment) noexcept(…)

Attempts to allocate the given size, and returns the information of said allocation.

Parameters
size in Amount you wish the allocate (this will be the minimum allocated size)
alignment in Special alignment override for the given allocation
Returns alloc_results<void>

Note

the alignment override cannot override the backing resource's own requirements, instead the allocation will try to satisfy both (or fail the allocation). For example, if the resource requires 4 byte alignment, and the user requests 6 byte alignment, the least common alignment they both satisfy is 12 bytes.
